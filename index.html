Problem & Solution

Diesel's Dirty Legacy

Diesel generators emit 0.8 kg CO2/kWh, cost €0.20/kWh, and are noisy. Romania’s 75% fossil-based emissions and rural off-grid reliance demand a cleaner alternative. EU’s Green Deal pushes for 45% renewables by 2030.

EcoGen’s Clean Power

Our 10kW portable solar generator delivers zero-emission power (0.05 kg CO2/kWh), saves €0.15/kWh, and operates silently. Built in Arad with AI optimization for 20% efficiency gains.

Market Opportunity

Romania’s renewable market grows at >3% CAGR, hitting €1.94B by 2030. EU’s €630M Horizon Europe fuels 600 GW solar by 2030. With 204,000+ prosumers and 79% off-grid demand growth, our TAM is €50B.
