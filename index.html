Problem & Solution

The Diesel Dilemma

Diesel generators: High emissions (0.8 kg CO2/kWh), fuel costs (€0.20/kWh), noise, and maintenance. Romania: 75% energy emissions from fossils; rural off-grid reliance. EU: Green Deal mandates 35% renewables by 2030.

Our Solution

10kW portable solar generator: Integrated panels (22% efficiency), 20kWh Li-ion battery, AI-optimized app. Zero emissions, €0.15/kWh savings, silent, modular. Manufactured in Arad for efficiency.
