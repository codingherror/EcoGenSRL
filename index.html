Problem & Solution

The Diesel Problem

Diesel generators produce 0.8 kg CO2 per kWh and cost €0.20 per kWh to operate, contributing heavily to Romania’s 75% fossil-based emissions.

Rural areas rely on these noisy, polluting units, while the EU’s Green Deal demands 45% renewables by 2030, creating an urgent need for alternatives.

EcoGen’s Solution

Our 10kW portable solar generator offers zero-emission power at 0.05 kg CO2 per kWh, saving €0.15 per kWh compared to diesel.

Manufactured in Arad with AI optimization, it delivers 20% efficiency gains and silent operation for residential and industrial use.

Market Opportunity

Romania’s renewable market is growing at over 3% CAGR, reaching €1.94 billion by 2030, driven by €28.5 billion in PNRR funding.

The EU’s €630 million Horizon Europe program supports 600 GW of solar by 2030, with 204,000+ prosumers and 79% off-grid demand growth in Romania.

Our total addressable market spans a €50 billion portable power sector, with strong demand in agriculture and rural areas.
